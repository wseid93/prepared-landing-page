Our AI-powered system tailors meal recommendations by analyzing:

  • Your dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Fitness goals and activity levels
  • Taste preferences and past meal ratings
  • Nutritional requirements and macro targets

The more you use Prepared, the smarter it gets in refining your meal selections.

Can I customize my meal plan?

Absolutely! You have full control over your meal plan:

  • Choose your preferred chef and change on a weekly basis
  • Let the AI know how many meals you want, your budget, and macro targets
  • Specify ingredients you dislike or are allergic to
